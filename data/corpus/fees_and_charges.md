**doc_id:** FAC-2026  
**effective:** 2026-01-01  
**jurisdiction:** AU  
**classification:** internal-synthetic

## 1. Purpose

This schedule describes fee names and when they may apply. Authoritative dollar amounts for this
PoC are resolved by the deterministic fee engine (`fees.py`), not by free-form model generation.

## 2. Home Loan Standard — annual package fee

Fee code: `HL_STD_ANNUAL`  
Applies: once per year on the package anniversary while the package remains open.  
Staff explanation: tell the customer the fee name and that the amount comes from the controlled schedule.
Waivers require team-leader approval outside this assistant.

## 3. Dissatisfaction about fees

If a customer disputes a fee, follow the Complaints Handling Procedure. Do not promise reversal.
