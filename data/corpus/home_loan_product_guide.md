## 3.1 Standard Home Loan — overview

The Home Loan Standard product is a variable-rate owner-occupier loan for eligible personal customers.
Staff must not provide personal financial advice. Explain product features using this guide and refer
complex suitability questions to an accredited adviser where required by policy.

## 3.2 Eligibility (high level)

Applicants must be Australian residents for tax purposes, meet serviceability checks performed in the
lending system, and provide required identification documents. This guide does not replace credit policy.

## 3.3 Key features

- Offset account availability: Yes (Standard Offset)
- Redraw: Yes, subject to available redraw balance
- Repayment frequency: Weekly, fortnightly, or monthly
- Staff must cite this section when describing features to customers

## 3.4 Annual fee

The annual package fee for Home Loan Standard is defined in the Fees & Charges Schedule.
Staff must use the controlled fee table / schedule. Do not invent or round fees from memory.
If a customer requests a fee waiver, escalate to team leader — frontline staff cannot approve waivers
from this assistant.

## 3.5 Customer communications

Drafted explanations may be used as speaking notes. Final commitments (approvals, variations,
waivers) require the appropriate human authority and system of record update.
